Board Of Directors

Engr. Sayed Munsif Ali

CHAIRMAN AND CEO

A distinguished businessperson and perceptive entrepreneur, Engr.Syed Munsif Ali is the founder promoter of COMPATH Limited, a Computerized comprehensive Diagnostic Complex opts to do business by providing world-class revolutionized the healthcare and diagnostic service in Bangladesh. With his visionary leadership and keen business knowledge, Multiplan Limited drives forward to not only provide superior quality products, but to demonstrate unparalleled foresight by developing projects which are the epitome of modern architecture and comfortable living. Engr. Syed Munsif Ali completed his B.Sc Engineering (Civil) Degree at Rajshahi Engineering College with highest marks at 1973. Over the past three decades he has established Multiplan Limited as one of the leading corporate houses in Bangladesh, with investments in diversified sectors in Bangladesh. Engr. Syed Munsif Ali is a life fellow of the institution of Engineers, Bangladesh , life member of AOTS of Japan, life member , Narayangong Rifle Club and Manikgong Rifle Club and also a member of Dhaka Rifle Club and member of Kurmitola Golf Club and Dhaka Club. Engr. Syed Munsif Ali is the founding Chairman and Director of Golden Life Insurance Ltd. Bangladesh as well as the Director and Chairman of Risk Management Committee of the NRB Commercial Bank Ltd. Bangladesh.
