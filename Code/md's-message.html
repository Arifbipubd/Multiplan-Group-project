MESSAGE FROM THE MD

Shamimatun Nasim

Managing Director, Multiplan Group

As a service sector, prime efforts should be given to become affordable for all week of life to have own home. This can not be possible only in the private sector. It needs to be aided by the government. Then only real theme of the Real Estate Industry be true to its success. The Real Estate sector in Bangladesh has come a long way by becoming one of the faster growing market in the world. The growth of the industry mainly due to large population and limited land area, rising income level and rapid urbanization.

MULTIPLAN LIMITED was set up with a clear vision, is to be the leading provider in the country’s Real Estate Industry from 1998 through providing professional, accurate, and timely services to our clients. However, as visionary Company since inception as the leading organization in the industry and in the market, MULTIPLAN has outlined the future of Real Estate development by delivering outstanding service and value to our clients as well as unique investment opportunities and superior return to our investors. With this goal in mind, MULTIPLAN GROUP’s employees worked together to develop our business. Our business reputation is not only on sale based, it also on customers’ satisfaction. Although Real Estate Industry is passing a difficult time and the market is uneven, yet it is a great sector for the stake holders to take opportunity of ongoing policies. Our journey started with Engineering & Construction.

Today, the group has enlarged its business in heterogeneous sectors, these are-ENTERPRIZE OF MULTITRADE, MULTITRADE INTERNATIONAL, MULTIPLAN ROYAL PREMIER MEAT and many others. MULTIPLAN has its successful presence in every section. Due to our expansion of business created new employment opportunities also. Other entities of the Group also engaged in different type of business from Trading to Supply, Dredging, Construction to Energy Generation. In the last few years, MULTIPLAN has significantly strengthened its leading position in this sector by successfully completion of some notable projects and adding major new projects to its portfolio. It has successfully handed over one of the largest shopping mall in the country MULTIPLAN CENTRE at the busiest shopping hub New Elephant Road, Dhaka. With a view to provide excellence in living comfort and to solve the city’s present housing backlog, we have introduced one of the biggest apartment complex MULTIPLAN RED CRESCENT CITY comprising 1000 units of apartments and about 100,000 sft. commercial spaces with joint venture program with Bangladesh Red Crescent Society. We also successfully completed many other projects’ by namely- MULTIPLAN SADEQUES-Baridhara DOHS, MULTIPLAN GREEN VILLA-Green Road, MULTIPLAN DALE-Basundhara, MULTIPLAN ISNIRR-Basundhara, MULTIPLAN NTF-West Dhanmondi and many other ongoing or proposed projects we have in pipeline. Also to extend the housing facility outside the Dhaka city we stared biggest apartment complex MULTIPLAN SHAHAJALAL CITY in the holy land of Hazrat Shahjalal (RA), the Sylhet. We successfully handover 50% apartment units within a short period, rest of the work is going on fast. Out of Dhaka city our another achievement is successfully handover our land project in Mymensingh is MULTIPLAN MODEL TOWN and MULTIPLAN MODELTOWER-1 and MULTIPLAN MODEL TOWER-2 are for commercial and residential spaces. Others are located at Basabo, Dhanmondi and Gulshan-2 of the capital city.

I will forever believe that buying a home is a great investment and security. In honor to peoples investment we are committed to bringing the best practices of the Real Estate Industry and to match it with the most advanced and relevant technologies to bring a complete solution to our clients. The passion and commitment of our team helped us garner many external accolades and recognition during the years. Their approach philosophy ensures clients’ demand which are important to each and every member of our organization. Their great dedication, professionalism and customer oriented performance have led the Company to the leading position of the industry.

We look forward to further cementing this reputation by seeking support from our clients, associates and stakeholders. We are united by a common culture of discipline, flexibility, and cooperation that makes us unbeatable in the market. It is crucial that we sustain the present efforts to be able, as fast as possible, to return to the path of prosperity and development. I also wish to express my most heartfelt thanks to each and every one of you, the loyal team workers and professionals. Especially to those who are our most honorable customers and stakeholders for your continued trust and support.


With Sincere Thanks
